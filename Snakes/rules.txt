Snakes - THE RETRO VERSION

1. Your snake's objective is to keep eating

2. your score will increase by 10 points when you eat 1 gem
   and by 20 if you eat a golden gem

3. if you hit the wall or eat yourself, you are out

4. press any arrow to start the game

5. the snake can go up, down, left or right

6. press w & s to increase and decrease speed

6. press enter to pause, and space to unpause

	make your high score!
